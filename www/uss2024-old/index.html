Welcome!

Welcome to the website of the UCREL NLP Summer School in Lancaster, UK.

The UCREL NLP Summer School 2024!

UCREL NLP Summer School 2024 is a three-day immersion in the world of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computational Linguistics, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence, hosted at Lancaster University's cutting-edge InfoLab21 facilities from July 24th to 26th. With a program featuring distinguished speakers, engaging hands-on tutorials, and a focus on early-stage PhD students with a background in NLP and Python programming, this event offers a unique blend of learning, networking, and hands-on experience with big data. Attendees can also participate in a poster session to showcase their research and receive feedback, and a mini hackathon with prizes for collaborative teams. Early bird registration, available until March 1st, 2023, offers a discounted rate of £295, covering accommodation, meals, and refreshments. Spaces are limited, so seize this opportunity to join us for the UCREL NLP Summer School 2024 and advance your expertise in the field.

Why Join Us?

🌞 UCREL NLP Group: UCREL, the University Centre for Computer Corpus Research on Language, is a dynamic and diverse group comprising six dedicated academics, alongside more than 20 passionate researchers and PhD students. We are united by our unwavering commitment to exploring various Natural Language Processing (NLP) related topics across an impressive array of 20 languages from different corners of the world. With a rich blend of research spanning Computing, Finance, Linguistics, Health, and Medicine, our group is at the forefront of cutting-edge NLP research and applications. Our collective dedication to excellence is evident in our remarkable output of over 1600 papers across diverse multidisciplinary fields, reinforcing our position as leading contributors in the realm of Natural Language Processing (NLP) research. Moreover, our groundbreaking research has not only made a significant impact but also received widespread recognition, amassing over 23,000 Google Scholar citations, underscoring our enduring influence and relevance in advancing the field of NLP. For more information about our work and initiatives, please visit our website at https://ucrel.lancs.ac.uk/.

🗣️ Engage with Thought Leaders: Our program features renowned speakers, and engaging tutorials on cutting-edge NLP topics. Designed to cater to the needs of PhD students in their 1st or 2nd year, we expect attendees to have some experience in NLP and Python programming. The summer school includes a poster session, where attendees can showcase their research and receive valuable feedback from experts in the field. Additionally, we are hosting a mini hackathon, where participants collaborate in teams, with prizes and certificates awaiting the victorious group.

🖥️ Hands-On Tutorials: Elevate your skills through interactive tutorials led by experts in the field. Dive into practical sessions in our brand new cutting-edge labs where you will work on several state of the art NLP topics, gaining valuable experience that you can apply immediately.

🌐 Global Networking: Connect with fellow NLP enthusiasts attending our summer school. Explore the chance to expand your professional circle, work together on projects, and establish lasting connections with individuals who share your enthusiasm for language technology.
